I am a freelance illustrator based in Yangon, Myanmar mainly working on digital medium to create colourful and eye catching characters and illustrations.

My works usually explicit very little rendering with cute and colourful art style. Textures are major elements of my art portfolio intending to highlight the very feeling of traditional color-pencil art.

I work with both commercial and private clients and has two years experience of working with commissions. I draw under the name of “Planet Rabbit” which is inspired from her favourite show “Sailor Moon”.
